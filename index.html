About Lauren

Lauren specializes in inspired web development and design for small businesses. Lauren’s years as a web designer gave her an eye for beauty. Her techinical background started in physics and bloomed her career as a full-stack web developer and designer. Lauren is a one stop shop for both design and development. With the eye of a designer and the ability of a developer, your vision will come to life!

Design & Development Portfolio

Lauren’s past clients include cannabis brands, yoga teachers, travel companies, skincare lines, musicians, artists, and more. Whatever your business, we think of designing a website as a collaborative process of co-creation. Lauren strives to keep your work with her light-hearted, joyful, and efficient. The design process should be organized and structured enough to keep it stress free, yet loose enough to allow creativity to work its magic!
